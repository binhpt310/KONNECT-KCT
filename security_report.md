# KCT Security Incident Summary (Minimum Disclosure)

This document provides a minimal official summary  
of the KCT security incident that occurred on  
December 13, 2024.

---

##  1. Incident Summary

- Date of Incident: December 13, 2024  
- Description:  
  A portion of project-managed wallets were compromised  
  by an external attacker, resulting in unauthorized transfers.

- Affected Amount:  
  3,610,499,984 KCT

- Immediate Actions:  
  - Exchanges (HTX, Gate.io, GOPAX) were immediately notified.  
  - Temporary suspension of deposits/withdrawals was requested.  
  - All affected wallets were locked and isolated.

- Investigation:  
  The case is under official investigation  
  by Korean cybercrime authorities.

---

##  2. Project Response

- Consolidated operational wallets into a new  
  secure master reserve structure  
- Performed third-party audits (Beosin)  
- Closed all high-risk wallets  
- Continued cooperation with partner exchanges

---

##  3. Compensation Policy

- 1:1 Token Swap Program (Started January 23, 2025)  
  - Below 100,000 KCT: Auto-swap  
  - Above 100,000 KCT: Verification required  
  - Suspected wallets:  
    Subject to investigation results before compensation

---

##  4. Current Status

- All compromised amounts have been fully tracked and categorized  
- New ecosystem rebuilding has begun under the KINC platform  
- Additional updates will be disclosed as required
